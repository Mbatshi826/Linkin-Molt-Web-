Our Services

At Linkin Molt Pty (LTD), we offer a wide range of services to cater to all your preferences and needs. We provide these services at our store:

  • Orange Money Services:

     You can deposit or withdraw money from your Orange money account at our store, But also when we are carrying out this process we humbly ask our customers to bring their national IDs or passports for Identification purposes.


  • FNB Cashplus Services:

     You can also withdraw money from your own fnb account, e-wallet or even sending the cash via e-wallet and pay2cell services at our Bhokamano Store.
    From oil changes to engine repairs, we've got you covered.


  • Smart Switch:

     We also have the Smart Switch machine for the people who are less fortunate and destitute, they can also purchase goods at our store.


  • Flexible Payment Options:

     We provide flexible payment opions to enable customers to purchase their desired goods from our stores even if they dont have cash at hand.
    As they can use their smart switch cards, or transfer the amount using orange money or pay2cell.


  • Customer Support:

     Your satisfaction is our top priority.
    Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is available to answer your questions and provide assistance whenever you need it. And also free delivery is offered to customers who buy in bulk but live very far from the store.
